T-money Card: Your Key to Seoul Transportation

The T-money card is essential for navigating Seoul's extensive public transportation system.

Where to Buy

Purchase your T-money card at any subway station ticket machine or convenience store like 7-Eleven, CU, or GS25.

How to Load Money

Add money at ticket machines, convenience stores, or through mobile apps. Minimum charge is usually 1,000 KRW.

Using on Buses

Tap when boarding and when getting off. The fare is automatically calculated based on distance traveled.

Subway Benefits

Enjoy discounted fares and seamless transfers between different subway lines across Seoul metropolitan area.
